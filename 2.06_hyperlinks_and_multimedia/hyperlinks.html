Hyperlink and their types

Absolute references

Absolute references (sometimes called external links) connect to pages in other domains by providing full link to external resource page.

  • Google.com
  • Facebook.com
  • Instagram.com

Relative references

Relative references (sometimes called internal links) connect to pages in our domain by providing filepath to desired resource.

  • Click here to open red orange pic

Images as links

More and more people are moving away from textual prompts to ones that are graphics based.
Click on orange pic to view it new page.

Embedded links

Embedded links do not connect to external or internal pages or resources. Instead, they provide means to jump to certain points on already opened page as a means of navigation.
